Landmark ruling on ancestral property rights and the validity of oral partitions. The Court held that oral partition, if proved by long possession, is valid even without registered documents.

Venkata Rao vs. State of Andhra Pradesh

Supreme Court
2022 (2022) 8 SCC 567 Land Dispute

Clarified the limitation period for challenging mutation orders under the AP Revenue Act. Held that 3-year limitation applies from the date of knowledge, not from the date of mutation entry.

High Court AP
