Frequently Asked

Common questions about our legal services

What types of cases do you handle?

We specialize in Land & Property Law, Revenue Matters (ROR corrections, Webland issues, 1B/Adangal corrections), Civil Litigation, Property Registration, Title Verification, and Cheque Bounce cases under Section 138 NI Act.

How much do you charge for a consultation?

Initial consultations are available at nominal fees. Exact charges depend on the complexity of your case. We maintain full transparency with no hidden costs. Use our AI Cost Estimator tool for an instant estimate.

Which courts do you practice in?

We practice across all courts in Andhra Pradesh including District Courts, Mandal Revenue Offices (MRO), RDO Courts, and the High Court of Andhra Pradesh. We also handle Tribunal matters and represent clients at Tahsildar offices.

Are the AI tools on this website free?

We offer a Trial Version of our AI Legal Tools for generating preliminary drafts and estimates. For legally valid, court-ready documents and full reports, a paid consultation is required.

How can I schedule an appointment?

Simply call us at +91 7036666089 or send a WhatsApp message. You can also use the contact form on this website. We typically respond within 2-4 hours during business hours.
